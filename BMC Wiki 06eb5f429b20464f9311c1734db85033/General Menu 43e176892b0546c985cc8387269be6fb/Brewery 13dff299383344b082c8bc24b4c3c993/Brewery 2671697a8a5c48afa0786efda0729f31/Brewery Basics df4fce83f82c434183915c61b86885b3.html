Brewing :

Brewing is a process that can be as simple as putting ingredients in a cauldron or as complicated as a three step system, allowing for the making of drinks ranging from Vodka to Iced Coffee. The process is very much discovery focused, and quick mastery of this art isn't to be taken lightly. Best of luck and happy brewing !

I. Fermenting :

The first step in brewing consists of Fermenting the fresh Ingredients.

  1. Place Cauldron over a Fire or other heat source such as a campfire or Magma block
  1. Fill the cauldron with water using a bucket
  1. Add Ingredients in to the cauldron (this is done by right clicking with the ingredient on the caldron)
  1. Wait for the recipe required time while the ingredients ferment
  1. Decant into glass bottles (three is standard for one cauldron)

II. Distilling :

Some Recipes don't require distilling but those that do are known as spirits.

  1. Put the bottle with ferment into the brewing stand
  1. Put glowstone dust to act as a filter on top into the brewing stand (The glowstone used to filter will not be consumed during the prosses)
